Hey There!

Welcome to House on the Hill Childcare where your child will learn, create, laugh, and grow! We are a family owned and operated daycare facility nestled in the Hills of East Nashville. We strive to provide families with quality, and affordable care in an "in-home" style environment. We are a small, independent daycare that takes pride in providing unique activities and being close knit with our families. Our program will accomodate children ages 6 weeks to 12 years of age.

Hope to see you soon!

Meet Miss Leni & Ms. B

Miss Leni's Bio more_vert
Miss Leni's Bio close

Le'Nisha Walker is the Owner/Operator of House on the Hill Childcare and is fondly called Miss Leni by both children and parents. Le'Nisha's professional background includes childcare and medical care giving not only in Indiana but Nashville, Tennessee as well. Le'Nisha is a registered medical assistant and certified in pediatric first aid/CPR/AED procedures.
Le'Nisha has a strong passion for teaching and caring for children. "Train up a child in the way he should go and when he is old he will not depart from it." (Proverbs 22:6)

Ms. B's Bio more_vert
Ms. B's Bio close

Beverly Walker-Qualls (Ms. B) is the Owner/Administrator at House on the Hill Childcare. After spending many years in the healthcare and human service industry, the philosophy of House on the Hill Childcare was established. Beverly is licensed in the medical field, certified in CPR, and holds a degree in Human Service Management.

Beverly is passionate about childcare and is a firm believer it takes a village to raise children.
